Description

Hello, I am a dedicated Chrome Extension developer with over a year of experience in creating innovative and user-friendly extensions. Having successfully developed and launched more than 10 extensions, I have honed my skills in designing, coding, and testing extensions that cater to a wide range of users.

In my career, I have created both public and private extensions, with the public ones gaining significant traction. Recently, one of my public extensions surpassed 50+ downloads within just two weeks of its release, demonstrating my ability to develop solutions that resonate with users and address their needs.
